Haida Gwaii: Land of Ancient Stories

Journey to the ancestral lands of the Haida people. Experience traditional storytelling, witness master carvers at work, and explore pristine coastal landscapes that have inspired our culture for generations.

Great Bear Rainforest: Sacred Wilderness

Walk with Indigenous guides through ancient forests, learn about our sustainable practices, and discover the spiritual significance of the rare Spirit Bear in First Nations culture.

Nk'Mip: Desert Wisdom

Experience the unique culture of the Osoyoos Indian Band in Canada's only desert. Learn how our people have thrived in this distinctive landscape for countless generations.
